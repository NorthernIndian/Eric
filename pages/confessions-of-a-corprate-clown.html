Confessions of a Corporate Clown

Sorry to break it to you, but this story is not about that famous clown or his famous fast food chain. It’s about new adventures and jumping into the unknown. It’s about trying to have fun and laugh when life gets tough. It’s about bringing a few more smiles into the world. It’s about loneliness. It’s about fear. It’s about freedom. It’s about who we think we are versus who we really are. How do we navigate through the day while we glue-on then peel-off mask after mask? Who are we dressing up for? Why the costumes? Why the voices? Trying to find our way back after losing our way is bold. Shedding skins we don’t need anymore is scary. Knowing we have to drop something in order to pick something up is uncomfortable. You ever try forcing yourself into a medium-sized shirt when you know you're a large? There is a childlike playfulness we all have deep inside of us. I truly believe that. And if yours is sleeping right now, I hope our show can wake it up today. Or at the very least, get it stirring.
